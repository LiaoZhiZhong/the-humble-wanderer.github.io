---
title: "Updating your address"
categories:
  - Relocating
---

When relocating, there's a few places where you'll need to update your address:
- Work
- Banks
	- E.g., ANZ, Commbank, Westpac, NAB, etc
- Immigration department if you're a temporary resident
- Mygov (ATO will automatically be updated)
- Super
- Your investment stuff e.g., Betashares for ETFs
